FAQs



Overview

What can I do with the FrrndLease service?

FrrndLease provides two complimentary services.
a)Storage Service: It allows you to store household things like kids toys, kitchen appliances and vacation items like cameras, that you use rarely or seasonally with trusted members for FREE.
b)Library Service: It also allows members to rent/borrow/lease household things belonging to friendly members (or our partner merchants) for 'credits' earned on the platform.

Why do you call it 'friend' + 'lease'?

The service relies on peer to peer borrowing and lending within a trusted circle of friends and 'friends of friends', not strangers. Also, all the transactions are leases with the explicit agreement that items always belong to their owners and are just passing through the hands of their friends.

Store

What do I pay to Store things with FrrndLease?

The storage service is free for verified members.

What is a verified member?

A member with a valid address proof uploaded to your profile and living in a Prime location is a verified member.

What is a Prime location?

This is the location where FrrndLease does delivery. Currently in Pune.

What if I am not in a Prime location?

You can still use the service with the Selfie Plan, where members can Pickup and Return your things without FrrndLease managing their logistics.

Where will my things be stored?

You things will be stored with other verified members on FrrndLease.

Can I decide who to store my things with?

Yes, you have the final say on who stores your things, based on the requests that you have received.

Can I choose to not store my things with anyone?

Yes, it is absolutely your decision. Only verified members can make requests for your things. We make the requestors profile and past standing on FrrndLease available for you to make a good decision.

How long can I store things with members?

You decide the duration of the lease. Typically, it is 1 month; and can be renewed as many times as needed by you and the Leasee.

How much does it cost the Leasee to store my things?

The Leasee uses his 'Credits' to store your things. No money is involved.

What is the Leasee loses or damages my things?

The Leasee promises to take good care of things. Her reputation on FrrndLease is based on that! However, there can be cases when things get damages or lost. In those cases, we charge the Insurance Amount of the item from her which you receive.

What is an items Insurance Amount ?

This is the reasonable replacement value of the item. While we expect the community of friends to all be good citizens, sometimes stuff happens. In those cases, we charge the Leasee this amount to compensate you for your item.

When can I get my stored things back?

The Lease can be closed at any time by any party. If you need the item earlier than planned, you simply close the lease and the item will be delivered back to you in the next delivery cycle.

What is the delivery cycle?

Deliveries are batched and typically take place on the next Sunday.


Lease

Who can lease things from FrrndLease?

Any verified member on FrrndLease can lease things from other verified members.

How long can I lease things?

It depends on the lease 'term' of the item set by the Owner. Typically, it is 1 month.

How much does it cost to lease an item?

You use 'Credits' to lease items. No money is involved.

Who delivers the items?

FrrndLease delivers items for all Prime members. Ie. All members that are verified and live in a Prime location.

How much does the delivery cost?

There is a flat Rs. 100/- fee to deliver the item for all Prime members.

Can I Pickup and Return the items myself to avoid the delivery fee?

Absolutely! All Prime members have a choice to Pickup items themselves or use FrrndLease to manage the logistics.

Do I have to pay a deposit to lease things?

No, you do not have to pay a deposit at the time of leasing things.

What is the 'Insurance' amount that I see on every item?

This is the 'virtual' deposit that you would pay to lease the item. It is not taken from you in good faith. However, if you damage or lose the item leased, this amount will be recovered from you.

How many credits do I need to Lease different items?

Since this is a Library, all items need the same number of credits.


Plans

What is the Selfie Plan?

This is the default self-service plan when you sign up. It is for members that want to try out the service, without becoming Verified members. It is also for members outside the current Prime locations to try out the service. FrrndLease does not manage logistics for leases between two Selfie users, but acts like a facilitator.

What is the Prime Plan?

The Prime plan allows you to Store and Lease items with friends and members and avail of Prime delivery in certain locations. We Pickup and Return items and manage the logictics for you. There is a nominal delivery fee paid by the Leasee for Pickup and Return of the item. The Owner does not pay any fee.

What is the Uber Plan?

The Uber Plan allows members to lease items from our sponsored partners and merchants. It increases the selection of items at their disposal. It is a subscription plan that has free delivery in Prime locations.


Credits

How do I earn credits?

You earn credits by doing good things!
a)By offering your things to members (Post)
b)By referring friends to become members (Invite)
c)By leasing things to members (Lease)
d)In addition, you get credits when you sign up with FrrndLease (Sign Up)

What can I use the credits for?

You use the credits to rent/borrow/lease things that you need by leasing it from members. All rentals need the same credits.

What if I don’t have enough credits?

You can earn credits (see above). Alternatively, you can buy credits (coming soon).

What can I do with all my credits?

You can avail Rewards from our partner merchants to encash your Credits outside of the FrrndLease ecosystem. (coming soon).
