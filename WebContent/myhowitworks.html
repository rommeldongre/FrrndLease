Reduce. Reuse. Feel Good!


fRRndLease is an easy to use application that will help you store, share and use the things you want with trusted friends and family.

Store


Have a camera that you love, but don't use frequently? Need to create space, but can't part with that sentimental sofa? Wondering what to do with that brand new bicycle, that the kids have outgrown? With fRRndLease you can store them all with friends that will use your things gently and return them on your terms with a whole lot of appreciation. Go on, offer it to your own Friend Store!

Try


Need to try a treadmill before making a purchase? Buying a wetsuit for that scuba vacation? Want a sofa-bed just for your in-laws visit? With frrndLease you can poll your friends for things they don't use frequently, have outgrown its use or want to pass onto worthy owners. Chances are they have that, and they would be happy to lend it to you. Go on, you'll be surprised at the things that your friends possess, but never use!

Lease


frrndLease takes the awkwardness of lending your stuff to friends with its friendly but bullet-proof agreement between friends. So you don't have to lose your friendship over an old sofa.
