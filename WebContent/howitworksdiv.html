How it Works

1. Post

Offer your less-used items to The Friend Store by creating a simple posting. Earn credits for every post!

2. Request

Receive Requests for your posted items from members, who use their credits to try your items at your terms.

3. Lease

Grant a Lease to a member of your choice, and earn even more credits. We will pick up the item from your doorstep and deliver it.

4. Return

When the lease term finishes, we will bring the item back to your doorstep.
