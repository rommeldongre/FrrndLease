How it Works

Make Space

1. Post Items

Offer your less-used items to The Friend Store by creating a simple posting. Earn credits for every post!

2. Receive Requests

Receive Requests for your posted items from members, who use their credits to try your items at your terms.

3. Grant Lease

Grant a Lease to a member of your choice, and earn even more credits. We will pick up the item from your doorstep and deliver it.

4. Get Return

When the lease term finishes, we will bring the item back to your doorstep.

Try Things

1. Search

Search the The Friend Store for items that you need or want to try.

2. Send Request

Send Requests for your items of interest to the owner.

3. Start Lease

Once your Request is accepted by the owner, we will pick up the item from the owners and deliver it to you.

4. Return Item

When the lease term finishes, we will pick up the item from your doorstep, and return it to the owner.

Feel Good

Earn Credits

Earn credits for all your good deeds, and use them to lease the things you need.

  • +10 Free credits On Signup
  • +10 credits On Posting an Item
  • +10 credits To Lease to Member
  • +5 credits To Lease to Friend
  • -10 credits Lease from Member
  • -10 credits Lease from Friend

Get Rewards

Encash your Credits for Rewards from our partner Merchants.
