How it Works

1. Post Item

Offer your less-used items to The Friend Store by creating a simple posting. Earn credits for every posting!

2. Item Request

Receive Requests for your items from members.

3. Grant Lease

Grant a Lease to your member of choice. We will pick up the item from your doorstep and deliver it.

4. Return

When the lease term finishes, we will bring the item back to you.
