Benefits

FrrndLease allows you to offer your stuff to friends and members and lend it to them at your terms. So you don't have to lose your friendship over an old sofa.

Make Space

Offload your seasonal, rarely-used items with friends who will use them gently and return them on your terms with a whole lot of karma.

Try Things

Try out items before making a purchase or use them for those rare occasions by borrowing them from your friends.

Feel Good

Get the full experience of using stuff, without taxing our fragile world.
